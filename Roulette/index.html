How to Play Roulette

  1. Click any number, or any box at the bottom of the table, to place a bet (multiple bets allowed).
  2. Bottom boxes let you bet on groups: dozens (1st 12, 2nd 12, 3rd 12), columns (2 to 1), even/odd, red/black, and low/high (1 to 18, 19 to 36).
  3. Click SPIN to spin the wheel and launch the ball.
  4. If the ball lands on a number or group you bet on, you win!
  5. To play again, place new bets and spin.
